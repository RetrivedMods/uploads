The Evolution of Video Streaming Technology

Over the past two decades, video streaming has transformed the way we consume media. From the early days of buffering struggles to today's seamless 4K experiences, the technology has come a long way.

Advancements in compression algorithms, such as H.265 and AV1, have allowed high-definition content to be delivered at lower bitrates, saving bandwidth and improving accessibility globally.

Looking ahead, the integration of AI in streaming platforms promises personalized recommendations, automated captioning, and even interactive content that adapts to viewer preferences in real-time.
